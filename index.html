About Us

CSJM Polytechnic College is the one of the best polytechnic institute in UP. It was situated in AmbedkarNagar district on Faizabad road and started in 2005 by BTEUP, with 30 students in CS branch and 30 student in IT branch. Currently Institute has running four courses of diploma namely Electrical Engg, Electronics Engg, Computer science and Engg and Information Technology.  Welcome To CSJM

CSJM Ambedkarnagar was founded in 2012, with a mission to provide the country's youth with an opportunity to acquire an all-round education. Today, it is one of the landmark schools in central India, owing to the relentless efforts of its founder and her competent team.CSJM Ambedkarnagar COLLEGE endorses a holistic education model where each child has her or his own unique identity and self-esteem, which is valued and promoted. A high quality educational environment has the greatest positive effect on the life of a student. For our students we attempt to create an atmosphere of space, freedom, care and security in which they are nurtured to expand their horizons and grow. The intention of the school is to awaken the intelligence and the generosity of spirit in students so that they are able to meet an increasingly complex world without losing their identity. The cultivation of a global outlook, a love for nature and a concern for humanity are all part of our educational aims.

Principle's Message

CSJM Polytechnic College is the one of the best polytechnic institute in UP. It was situated in AmbedkarNagar district on Faizabad road and started in 2005 by BTEUP . Currently Institute has running four courses of diploma namely Electrical Engg, Electronics Engg, Computer science and Engg and Information Technology. College has its own campus and a huge playground. College has Labs where students can do practical and gain practical knowledge. We also provide games facilities to the students. Being a Principal of this esteemed college comes with equal responsibility as the honour. Our vision is to educate and empower Indian youth for technical skill. It is our constant effort to provide a conducive atmosphere and environment not only for academic excellence but also for the development of a high moral and spiritual character. We have a highly qualified and capable Faculty who not only teach, but also counsel and monitor. I believe that our students will have fruitful and meaningful stay on campus and that they will enrich campus life with their gifts and talents and in turn contribute their best to society.
